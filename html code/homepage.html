Why Us?

Generally , students need to refer to various resources like lecture notes , video lectures , exam question papers, E-books etc for their exam preparation. There are lots of materials present on the internet. Often students get confused in selecting the best and efficient resource which is as per their syllabus and can save their time. Through this project we aim to provide the one stop solution for all their semester resource needs. One can directly refer to it and find all the important resources for their ongoing semester.This can be very helpful to the students while preparing for their semester exams and it will save their time so that they can perform better in their exams. The main motive behind providing features is to optimize the time utilization of students and college hours.

The proposed system deals with environments confined to a specific college department. This project explores various opportunities to improvise the regular searches done by the students regarding lecture notes , video lectures , exam question papers, E-books etc. Finding the best study resources becomes a tedious task when handled manually.This can be simplified by putting all the resources together at one place. Through this approach, module wise clarity of subjects can be foretold for students. It can also be used for classifying different resources of a specific subject based on their availability and course outcomes.
